AUEN Music platform

Our product is a music player called AUEN. AUEN allows users to listen to music over the Internet from a website. The aim of the project is to create an alternative to such applications as Spotify, Yandex Music, Apple Music, etc. The site will feature mostly Kazakh artists. Not only modern performers, but also old, already forgotten, to remember and popularize our cool and soulful songs.
